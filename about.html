About Us

 

We are in field of Re-sharpening of HSS / CARBIDE Cutting Tool for last many years. We have a strength of 42 Grinding machines to give URGENT delivery of resharpened Cutting Tools. We have one unit at Goregaon, Mumbai with a capacity of about 28 machines and Second unit at Vasai, Dist Thane with a capacity of 14 machines. We provide Pick-up and Delivery of your Cutting Tools in Mumbai area. We do Re grinding, Grinding, Cutting tools Sharpening, Tools Sharpening, Tools Grinding of your Cutting Tools.
